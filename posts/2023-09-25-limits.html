Another intution for epsilon-delta

September 25, 2023

Consider the following lemma:

Lemma. If \(|x| < \varepsilon\) for all \(\varepsilon > 0\) then \(x = 0\).

And a corrolary of that lemma:

Corrolary. If \(|x - y| < \varepsilon\) for all \(\varepsilon > 0\) then \(x = y\).
